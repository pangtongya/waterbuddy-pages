水伴 隐私政策

最后更新日期：2026年6月25日

感谢您使用"水伴"应用。我们非常重视您的隐私和个人信息保护。本隐私政策旨在向您说明我们如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的个人信息。

一、我们收集的信息

1.1 您主动提供的信息

  • 宠物信息：您为虚拟宠物设置的名字、选择的宠物类型和皮肤
  • 个人设置：您设置的每日饮水目标、提醒时间、提醒间隔等偏好设置
  • 饮水记录：您手动记录的饮水数据（时间、饮水量、杯型等）

1.2 自动收集的信息

  • 健康数据：在您授权的前提下，我们会从 Apple 健康（HealthKit）读取和写入饮水数据，以便更好地同步您的饮水记录
  • 设备信息：设备型号、操作系统版本、应用版本号等技术信息，用于排查问题和优化体验

1.3 我们不收集的信息

  • 我们不会收集您的真实姓名、身份证号、手机号、邮箱等个人身份信息
  • 我们不会收集您的位置信息
  • 我们不会收集您的通讯录、相册内容（除非您主动保存分享图片到相册）

二、我们如何使用信息

  • 提供核心功能：记录您的饮水数据、计算每日进度、生成统计报表
  • 发送提醒通知：根据您设置的提醒时间，发送喝水提醒
  • 宠物养成系统：根据您的饮水情况，更新宠物状态和成长进度
  • 数据同步：在您授权的前提下，与 Apple 健康应用同步饮水数据
  • 改进产品：通过匿名的使用数据，优化应用功能和用户体验

三、信息的存储与安全

  • 您的所有数据都存储在您的设备本地（通过 iOS 原生的 UserDefaults 和 文件系统）
  • 我们不会将您的个人数据上传到任何服务器
  • 我们使用 iOS 系统级别的数据保护机制来保障数据安全
  • 您可以随时通过应用内的"重置所有数据"功能删除全部数据

四、HealthKit / 健康应用 数据使用说明

  • 我们仅在您明确授权后才会访问 Apple 健康数据
  • 我们只会读取和写入"饮水量"（Dietary Water）这一项数据
  • 健康数据仅用于在应用内展示和同步，绝不会被用于其他目的
  • 您可以随时在系统"设置" → "隐私与安全性" → "健康"中撤销授权

五、通知权限

  • 我们会请求通知权限，用于发送喝水提醒
  • 您可以随时在系统设置中关闭通知权限
  • 关闭通知权限不会影响应用的其他功能使用

六、应用内购买

  • 应用内购买由 Apple App Store 处理
  • 我们不会收集您的支付信息，所有交易都通过 Apple 的安全支付系统完成
  • 购买记录会保存在您的 Apple ID 账户中

七、儿童隐私

我们的应用面向所有年龄段用户。我们不会故意收集 13 岁以下儿童的个人信息。如果您认为我们可能收集了儿童的信息，请联系我们，我们会立即删除。

八、您的权利

  • 访问权：您可以在应用内查看自己的所有饮水记录和设置
  • 删除权：您可以随时通过"设置" → "数据管理" → "重置所有数据"删除全部数据
  • 导出权：您可以将饮水数据导出为 CSV 格式文件
  • 撤回同意：您可以随时在系统设置中撤回健康数据、通知等授权

九、隐私政策的更新

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。更新后的政策会在应用内和本页面公布，重大变更会通过应用内通知告知您。

十、联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问、意见或建议，请通过以下方式联系我们：

  • 邮箱：pangtong@example.com
  • 应用内反馈：设置 → 关于 → 意见反馈

WaterBuddy Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 25, 2026

Thank you for using WaterBuddy. We take your privacy and personal information protection very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Pet Information: The name you give your virtual pet, the pet type and skins you choose
  • Personal Settings: Your preferences such as daily water goal, reminder times, and reminder intervals
  • Water Records: Water intake data you manually record (time, amount, cup type, etc.)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Health Data: With your authorization, we read from and write to Apple Health (HealthKit) water data to better sync your water intake records
  • Device Information: Technical information such as device model, OS version, and app version for troubleshooting and experience optimization

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your real name, ID number, phone number, email, or other personally identifiable information
  • We do not collect your location information
  • We do not collect your contacts or photo album contents (except when you actively save share images to your photo album)

2. How We Use Information

  • Core Features: Recording your water data, calculating daily progress, and generating statistics
  • Reminder Notifications: Sending water drinking reminders based on your set reminder times
  • Pet Raising System: Updating pet status and growth progress based on your water intake
  • Data Sync: Syncing water data with Apple Health app with your authorization
  • Product Improvement: Optimizing app features and user experience through anonymous usage data

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All your data is stored locally on your device (via iOS native UserDefaults and file system)
  • We do not upload your personal data to any server
  • We use iOS system-level data protection mechanisms to ensure data security
  • You can delete all data at any time through the "Reset All Data" feature in the app

4. HealthKit / Health App Data Usage

  • We only access Apple Health data after your explicit authorization
  • We only read and write "Dietary Water" data
  • Health data is only used for display and syncing within the app, and will never be used for other purposes
  • You can revoke authorization at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health

5. Notification Permission

  • We request notification permission to send water drinking reminders
  • You can turn off notification permissions in System Settings at any time
  • Turning off notifications will not affect other app functionality

6. In-App Purchases

  • In-app purchases are processed by Apple App Store
  • We do not collect your payment information — all transactions are completed through Apple's secure payment system
  • Purchase history is saved in your Apple ID account

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we may have collected information from a child, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

8. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your water records and settings within the app
  • Deletion: You can delete all data at any time through Settings → Data Management → Reset All Data
  • Export: You can export your water data as a CSV file
  • Withdraw Consent: You can revoke health data, notification, and other authorizations in System Settings at any time

9. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be published within the app and on this page. Material changes will be notified through in-app notifications.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, comments, or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: pangtong@example.com
  • In-app feedback: Settings → About → Feedback
